My Name is Arys Aung

I'm a recent High School graduate excited to attend college this upcoming Fall! I've dedicated my blog to my number one role model: Kanye West. I've gotten the notion from (a lot of) others that Kanye may not be the most humbling individual to idolize... but I see things from a very different perspective. Where people see an ego, I see a hard-earned persona; pretentiousness, I see self-confidence. Kanye is humble, what he isn't is self-depreciating. In my blog, I dissect the message behind the most anticipated album of the year: The Life Of Pablo.

HTML stands for Hyper Text Markup Language, and it's essentially compromised of two elements. Taglines and Regular Text.

Tags or Regular Text

Tags always exist in pairs and are written as "< p > ... < /p >." The regular text exists in between the tags, which serve as your titles, paragraphs, and headings. The tags serve as instructions.
